# BreathClean
AI-Powered Personal Air Quality Intelligence Device

BreathClean is a portable device that monitors personal air pollution exposure in real time.  
Unlike traditional AQI apps that show city averages, BreathClean measures the air an individual actually breathes and converts sensor data into meaningful health insights.

---

## The Problem
Air pollution is one of the largest invisible health threats.  
City AQI apps show average pollution levels but cannot capture hyper-local exposure that changes from street to street.

---

## Our Solution
BreathClean integrates multiple environmental sensors with edge processing to detect pollutants such as PM2.5, PM10, VOCs and ozone.  
The device calculates AQI on-device and sends the data to a mobile application for visualization and health insights.

---

## Key Features
• Real-time PM2.5 / PM10 monitoring  
• VOC and ozone detection  
• On-device AQI calculation  
• Bluetooth connectivity with mobile app  
• Personal exposure tracking  
• Health score and smart recommendations
